preface⌗

a large reason as to why i wanted to create a collection of notes, information, and references on decompilers is primarily due to the fact that its such an obscure craft.

i have personally never known of any class of software that was as difficult to find resources on as decompilers. hell even the documentation for open source decompilers like ghidra and snowman are lackluster.

however, there is not a single tutorial on the internet on how to actually write a machine code decompiler. you would think that there would be plenty of tutorial on this subject given the amount of compiler development tutorials that exist; but to this day i have not found any.

so ive decided that ill be the self proclaimed “first” to tackle such an obscure and complex piece of software, and hopefully write a coherent and self contained tutorial on decompiler development.

these are the notes and references that ive collected, if you want to view the tutorial, please view the “Decompiler Development” series within my posts (if they are available at the time of viewing this).
